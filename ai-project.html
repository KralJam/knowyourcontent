The AI project.

To demonstrate the way that people can be misled by AI created social media and AI created news, we’ve created a news site that is 100% fiction. The articles are AI generated. The images are AI generated. These articles use generative AI and have no basis in fact or research. The system uses the lede from news sources and then creates its own articles from those ledes. This creates a resemblance to current events, but has zero research or fact checking.

Once these AI articles are created, then social media posts are created to disseminate the articles to an appropriate audience.

The aim is for a post to go viral, with the mainstream, legitimate media picking up the article and either quoting it or reposting it, then for this reposting to be revealed to be the media quoting a fictitious news source.

This project is a social experiment, an art project, with the aim of demonstrating how without regulation on social media and news media, we can all fall for AI fantasy.

We need our governments to act, we need to know what we are consuming. We need social media sites to flag AI content, news sites to state how they use AI in what they write. We need to know what is real and what isn’t. Without this, we are lost.
